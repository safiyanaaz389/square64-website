  • About us

    Square 64 Capital Advisors LLP  offers Portfolio Management Services, based out of  Mumbai. Square 64 has been ideated on the concept of ‘Value Investing’. It follows the simple strategy of investing in the right publicly listed companies, and then holding tight till the markets play out and the stock achieves the desired results.

    The firm has three partners at its helm – Priyankar Sarkar, Ankit Poddar and Vishwanshu Agarwal. Each of the partners has more than a decade of experience in portfolio management, business development and operations management.
